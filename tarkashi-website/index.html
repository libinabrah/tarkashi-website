The Craft

What "Tarkashi" means

Tarkashi (താർകാശി) is a filigree technique from Kerala, where fine strands of metal are drawn, twisted and soldered into lace-like patterns — flowers, leaves, hearts and temple motifs that have decorated South Indian jewellery for generations.

Every piece in this collection is chosen to carry that spirit into Onam: warm gold tones, motifs drawn from nature, and details made to catch the light of the nilavilakku through an evening of celebration.

  • Traditional antique and dual-tone gold finishes
  • Set with coloured stones, CZ and enamel work
  • Sets designed to pair with mundu, settu saree and pavada
